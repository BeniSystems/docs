## What is Beni?

Beni is a conversational AI platform that handles customer conversations across chat, call, and SMS - so you never miss a lead or leave a customer waiting. It learns your business from your website or documents, and responds to customers on your behalf across multiple channels - without needing human staff for every conversation.
