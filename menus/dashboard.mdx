The Dashboard gives you a real-time overview of your workspace — active agents, conversation volume, resolution performance, and incoming leads.

<Info>We're continuously adding more insights and summaries to the Dashboard to help you make better decisions faster.</Info>

## Metrics Overview

At the top of the Dashboard, four key metrics give you an instant pulse on your workspace:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Active Agents" icon="robot">
    The number of agents currently active out of your total configured agents (e.g. **1/1**).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Conversations" icon="message">
    Total number of conversations handled by your agents in the selected time period. Includes a week-over-week comparison to show growth or decline.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Autonomously Resolved" icon="circle-check">
    The percentage of conversations resolved by your agents without a human handoff, along with the raw count (e.g. **7 of 7 resolved**).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Leads Captured" icon="bullseye">
    Total number of leads captured by your agents in the selected time period. Includes a week-over-week comparison.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Conversations Chart

The Conversations chart shows the volume of conversations over time. Use the time range selector (e.g. **Last 7 days**) to adjust the period displayed.

Use this chart to spot trends — peak hours, quiet periods, or spikes that may need attention.

## Leads

The Leads section surfaces the most recent inbound leads captured by your agents. Each lead shows:

- **Name** — the visitor's name or "Website Visitor" if unknown
- **Summary** — a brief description of the lead's intent (e.g. "The customer explicitly requested to book a call and provided contact details")
- **Temperature** — `HOT` or `WARM` based on the lead's intent and engagement level
- **Time** — how recently the lead came in

Click **View all** to see the full leads list, or click any individual lead to view the full conversation and contact details.

## Conversation Topic Breakdown

The Conversation Topic Breakdown chart shows how conversations are distributed across different topics (e.g. Booking, Support, General). This helps you understand what your visitors are most commonly asking about.

## Quick Action

Use the **+ Create Agent** button in the top right to set up a new agent directly from the Dashboard.
