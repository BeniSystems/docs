The Leads page gives you a consolidated view of every lead your agents have captured. Track contact details, lead status, AI summaries, and notes — all without needing to dig through individual conversations.

## Lead Summary Cards

At the top of the page, four summary cards give you a quick breakdown of your lead pipeline:

| Card | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Total** | Total number of leads captured across all agents |
| **Hot** | Leads with high intent (flame icon, red) |
| **Warm** | Leads with moderate interest (thermometer icon, yellow) |
| **Cold** | Leads with low engagement (snowflake icon, blue) |

## Filtering & Search

Use the filter bar to find specific leads:

| Filter | Options |
|--------|---------|
| **Search** | Find leads by name or keyword |
| **All Leads** | Filter by archive state — `All Leads`, `Active`, or `Archived` |
| **All Priorities** | Filter by lead priority |
| **All Purposes** | Filter by purpose (e.g. Booking, Quote, Support, General) |
| **All Statuses** | Filter by lead status (New, Qualified, Contacted, Nurturing, Lost) |
| **Starred** | Show only starred/bookmarked leads |
| **Export** | Download the current lead list |

## Lead List

The table shows all captured leads with the following columns:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Name** | Visitor's name, or "Website Visitor" if not identified. Avatar with initials shown if name is known. |
| **Type** | Lead temperature — `Hot`, `Warm`, or `Cold` |
| **Purpose** | The intent category assigned by the agent (e.g. `Booking`, `Quote`, `Support`, `General`) |
| **Status** | Current pipeline status of the lead |
| **Last Active** | How long ago the lead was last active |

Click any row to open the lead detail panel on the right.

## Lead Detail Panel

Clicking a lead opens a side panel with full details.

### Header

- **Name** — visitor's name and avatar
- **Star** — bookmark the lead for quick access
- **Captured by** — which agent site captured the lead
- **View conversation** — link to the original conversation
- **Archive / Restore** — archive an active lead to remove it from the default view, or restore an archived lead to make it active again

### Tags

Lead tags appear as pills (e.g. `Hot`, `Booking`). These are automatically assigned based on the conversation and can also be added manually from the Conversation Info panel.

### Contact Details

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Email** | Email address captured during the conversation |
| **Phone** | Phone number captured during the conversation |

### Metadata

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Created** | Date and time the lead was first captured |
| **Last Active** | How long ago the lead was last seen |
| **Status** | Current pipeline status, with a pencil icon to edit inline |

### AI Summary

An AI-generated summary of the lead's intent and key details from the conversation (e.g. *"The customer explicitly wants to book an appointment for tomorrow at 9 AM and provides contact details."*).

### Signals

Behavioral signals detected during the conversation. Shows *No signals detected* if none were identified.

### Notes

Freeform notes you can add to a lead. Click **+ Add** to open the **Add Note** dialog, type your note, then click **Save Note** (or press `⌘+Enter`). Notes appear here for future reference. Existing notes can also be deleted.

## Updating Lead Status

To change a lead's pipeline status, click the **pencil icon** next to the status in the detail panel. A modal will appear with the following options:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **New** | Freshly captured, not yet actioned |
| **Qualified** | Reviewed and confirmed as a genuine lead |
| **Contacted** | You have reached out to the lead |
| **Nurturing** | Actively being followed up |
| **Lost** | Lead did not convert |

Select the new status to apply it immediately.

---

## Archiving Leads

Archiving lets you declutter your lead list without permanently deleting leads.

- Click the **Archive** button in the top-right of the lead detail panel to archive a lead.
- Archived leads are hidden from the default **All Leads** view.
- To view archived leads, use the **All Leads** filter and select **Archived**.
- To restore an archived lead, open it and click **Restore** — it will return to the active list.
