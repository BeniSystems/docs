The Conversations page gives you full visibility into every interaction your agents have with visitors. Review transcripts, monitor leads, join live conversations, and manage conversation details — all from one place.

## Conversation List

The left panel lists all conversations. Each row shows:

- **Visitor name** — the person who started the conversation
- **Last message preview** — a snippet of the most recent message, prefixed with who sent it (e.g. `Agent:` or `Support:`)
- **Time** — how long ago the conversation was last active
- **Star** — bookmark important conversations for quick access

Conversations are paginated. Use the **Per page** selector (top right) to control how many appear per page, and **Previous / Next** to navigate.

### Filtering

Use the filter bar at the top to narrow down conversations:

| Filter | Options |
|--------|---------|
| **Search** | Find conversations by keyword |
| **Status** | All Status, Active, Waiting, Resolved, Abandoned |
| **Lead Type** | All Lead Types, Hot, Warm, Cold |
| **Priority** | All Priorities |

### Conversation Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Active** | Conversation is ongoing — agent or human is engaged |
| **Waiting** | Visitor is waiting for a response |
| **Resolved** | Conversation was successfully closed |
| **Abandoned** | Visitor left before the conversation was resolved |

### Lead Types

| Type | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| `Hot` | High intent — visitor showed strong interest or asked about pricing/booking |
| `Warm` | Moderate interest — engaged but not yet ready to convert |
| `Cold` | Low intent — minimal engagement |

## Conversation Detail

Click any conversation to open it in a three-panel view:

- **Left** — the conversation list (stays visible for quick switching)
- **Center** — the full chat transcript and reply area
- **Right** — the Conversation Info panel

### Chat Transcript

The center panel shows the full conversation thread between the visitor and the agent, with timestamps on each message. Visitor messages appear as dark bubbles on the left, and agent responses appear as lighter bubbles on the right.

### Resolving a Conversation

When a conversation is **Active**, a green **Resolve** button appears in the conversation header. Click it to mark the conversation as resolved and close it.

### Joining a Conversation

If a conversation is **Active** or **Waiting**, you can reply directly from the **Reply as Support Agent** input at the bottom of the transcript. Type your message and press **Enter** to send, or **Shift+Enter** for a new line.

<Info>Replies are only available for Active or Waiting conversations. Resolved and Abandoned conversations are read-only — a notice will appear at the bottom of the transcript.</Info>

## Conversation Info Panel

The right panel shows metadata and context for the selected conversation.

### Details

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Agent** | The Beni agent that handled the conversation |
| **# Messages** | Total number of messages exchanged |
| **Started** | Date and time the conversation began |
| **Ended** | Date and time the conversation ended (shown for resolved conversations) |

### AI Summary

Once enough context is available, an AI-generated summary of the conversation will appear here. For new or short conversations it will show *No summary available yet.*

### Tags

Click **+ Add** to tag a conversation (e.g. `hot-lead`, `follow-up`, `bug-report`). Tags appear as pills and help you categorize and search conversations.

### Lead Info

If the agent identified the visitor as a lead, their details appear here:

- **Name** — visitor's name, along with their lead temperature and category tags
- **Email** — email address if captured during the conversation
- **Phone** — phone number if shared during the conversation
- **View Lead Profile** — opens the full lead profile page

<Info>Contact details are captured automatically when a visitor provides them. You can prompt agents to collect this information through their system instructions.</Info>
