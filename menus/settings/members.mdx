The Members page lets you invite teammates to your workspace and manage their roles and access.

The **Seats** counter in the top right shows how many seats are used out of your plan's limit (e.g. `3 / 5`). The number of available seats varies by plan.

## Member List

The table shows all current members and pending invitations with the following columns:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Name** | Member's display name |
| **Email** | Member's email address |
| **Role** | `Owner`, `Admin`, or `Member` |
| **Status** | `Active`, `Deactivated`, or `Invitation Sent` |
| **Actions** | Context menu with available actions for that member |

---

## Roles

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full control over the workspace including billing and settings. Each organization has exactly one Owner. |
| **Admin** | Can manage agents, knowledge base, tools, and members. |
| **Member** | Standard access to workspace features based on permissions. |

<Info>Each organization can only have one Owner. The Owner account cannot be deactivated.</Info>

---

## Inviting a Member

Click **Invite Member** to open the invitation form.

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Name** | The person's name |
| **Email** | The email address the invitation will be sent to |
| **Role** | Select `Admin` or `Member` |

Click **Send Invitation** to send. The member will appear in the list with an **Invitation Sent** status until they accept.

---

## Member Actions

Click the **⋯** menu on any member row to see available actions. Actions vary based on the member's current status:

### Active members
- **Edit** — Change the member's role. Other profile fields will be editable soon.
- **Deactivate** — Disable the member's access to the workspace.

### Deactivated members
- **Edit** — Change the member's role.
- **Reactivate** — Restore the member's access.

### Pending invitations
- **Resend** — Send the invitation email again.
- **Cancel Invite** — Revoke the invitation.

<Warning>The Owner account cannot be deactivated.</Warning>
