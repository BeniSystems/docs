The Notifications page controls organization-wide email notification preferences.

<Info>These settings apply organization-wide. Only Admins and Owners can update these preferences.</Info>

---

## Escalation Alerts

Get notified when a conversation is escalated to a human agent.

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Email on Escalation** | Toggle to send an email whenever a conversation is escalated |
| **Recipient Email** | The email address that receives escalation notifications |

<Note>Escalation alerts will move to agent-level settings in a future update, so each agent can have its own notification configuration.</Note>

---

## Lead Notifications

Get notified by email when the AI identifies potential leads from conversations. Enable notifications per lead temperature:

| Lead Type | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Hot** | Pricing inquiries, availability checks, booking requests — ready to convert |
| **Warm** | Detailed service questions, real interest shown but no commitment yet |
| **Cold** | Casual browsing, vague questions, no clear purchase intent |

Set a **Recipient Email** to receive lead notifications. Emails are only sent for lead types that are toggled on.

---

Click **Save Changes** to apply your settings, or **Reset** to revert to the last saved state.
