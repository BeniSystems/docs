Configure your organization's profile and tenant-level defaults that apply across your entire workspace.

## Settings

- **Organization Name** — The display name of your organization
- **Timezone** — Default timezone used across the workspace
- **Default Locale** — Language and locale settings
- **Support Email** — Email address for support-related communications
- **Billing Email** — Email address for invoices and billing notifications
- **Website** — Your organization's website URL

<Info>These are tenant-level organization defaults and apply across your workspace.</Info>
