The Billing page shows your current subscription and lets you manage your plan.

## Current Subscription

The top section shows your active plan with:

- **Plan name** and status (`Active`)
- **Billing cycle** — Monthly or Annual
- **Renews on** — your next renewal date
- **Conversations used** — usage against your monthly limit (e.g. `26 / 2,000`)

### Conversation Overage

If you exceed your monthly conversation limit, you can enable **Conversation Overage** to allow conversations to continue beyond your plan's included amount. The overage rate is determined by your plan.

<Info>Conversation Overage is only available on paid plans.</Info>

To cancel your subscription, click **Cancel subscription** at the bottom of this section.
